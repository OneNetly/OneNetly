Refund policy

You can cancel your shared hosting plan for a full refund within the first 30 days. It is applicable (1) if you are experiencing technical difficulties with our server, (2) if a feature that we discussed in the package specification is missing, or (3) if you are experiencing any other server error that we are unable to resolve. If you cancel within 30 days, you will only receive a full refund for your shared hosting service. Because of the one-of-a-kind nature of their costs, most add-on products, such as domains, are not eligible for a money-back guarantee. If you cancel within 30 days of receiving your free domain, OneNetly, Inc will deduct a non-refundable domain name registration fee from your refund.

OneNetly, Inc does not provide refunds for shared or reseller hosting cancellations after 30 days. Furthermore, change of mind is not permitted within 30 days. There are no partial or full refunds for monthly services such as VPS/Dedicated servers. However, if you have subscribed to any of these services for more than one month, you will receive a full refund for any unused periods. Any special, customized, or temporary packs, such as student packs, are not refundable. There are no refunds for web services such as web design and development, web templates and themes, technical support, and so on.
